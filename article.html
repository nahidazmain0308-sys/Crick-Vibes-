Mustafizur Rahman Becomes the First Bangladeshi to Reach 400 T20 Wickets

By Cricvibes Desk | Published: 2026
Mustafizur Rahman celebrating his historic 400th T20 wicket.

Bangladesh pace sensation Mustafizur Rahman has etched his name into cricket history by reaching the remarkable milestone of 400 wickets in T20 cricket. Known for his deadly cutters and calm execution under pressure, Mustafizur continues to be one of the most feared bowlers in the shortest format.

From international matches to franchise leagues around the world, the left-arm pacer has consistently delivered match-winning performances. His ability to deceive batters with slower deliveries and sharp angles has made him a true T20 specialist.

This achievement highlights not only his longevity but also his adaptability across different conditions and competitions. For Bangladesh cricket, Mustafizur’s milestone is a proud moment and a symbol of the nation’s growing impact on global T20 cricket.

As he continues his journey, fans eagerly await more magical spells from the man famously known as “The Fizz.”
